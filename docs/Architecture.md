# Wells Family Legacy Archive

## Vision

The Wells Family Legacy Archive is a multi-generational digital archive designed to preserve the history of the Wells family for future generations.

Unlike a traditional family tree, the archive combines genealogy with photographs, stories, documents, historical records, timelines, and community contributions.

---

## Core Principles

- Family history should never be lost.
- Every person deserves their own permanent profile.
- Living relatives remain private by default.
- Every change is reviewable before publication.
- The archive should continue growing for generations.
